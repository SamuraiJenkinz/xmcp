## Operational Notes

- **ATLAS_UI=react** env var required at Flask startup to serve React SPA. Defaults to "classic" (Jinja2 templates). This is by design — dual-mode architecture for safe rollout.
- **Running badge excluded** from tool panels — blocking SSE architecture prevents showing "Running" state. Documented as known limitation in Phase 17; not a gap.
